Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

At PassVault Pro, your privacy and security are our foundational principles. We believe that your passwords and personal data belong exclusively to you. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to maintaining a completely zero-knowledge environment.

1. Zero Data Collection

PassVault Pro is fundamentally designed as a 100% offline password manager. We do not have servers, databases, or analytics trackers that monitor your usage. We collect absolutely zero personal data. There are no accounts to create, no emails to provide, and no telemetry collected.

2. Local Storage Only

All your passwords, usernames, secure notes, and secret keys are encrypted entirely on your device and stored locally within your browser's secure storage layer. This encrypted data never leaves your computer or phone. We do not have access to your master password or the contents of your vault.

3. Third-Party Services

Because PassVault Pro runs entirely in your browser offline, no third-party tracking scripts, ad networks, or analytics tools are embedded in the application. Your actions within the vault remain completely private.

4. Your Responsibilities

Since your data is stored locally, it is your responsibility to safely backup your vault and remember your master password. Because we have no servers and no access to your data, if you lose your master password, your data cannot be recovered by anyone, including our team.

5. Updates to this Policy

As we add new offline features, this policy may be updated. However, our core commitment to a serverless, zero-data-collection architecture will never change.
