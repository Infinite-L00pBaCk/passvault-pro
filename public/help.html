Frequently Asked Questions

Forgot Master Password?

PassVault Pro is 100% offline with zero-knowledge architecture. This means we cannot reset your password. If you lose your master password, your vault cannot be recovered.

Is my data in the cloud?

No. Your vault is encrypted and stored purely on your local device. No credentials ever leave your browser. You can manually export your vault to a JSON file to transfer devices.

Does it work on Mobile?

Yes! PassVault Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can click "Install" in your browser menu to save it directly to your home screen like a native app.
